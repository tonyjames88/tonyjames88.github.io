About Me

About Me

I’m a Software Engineer by profession, employed in the IT industry for over 7 years. I’ve worked on multiple technology stacks like Data Analytics, Database and DevOps across multiple domains. My latest engagement involves interfacing between different teams, to help get the product built, tested and deployed to production on a quick release cycle – a DevOps role. This provides quick feedback to developers to know how well their code merges with the main code, automated testing and prompt feedback via notifications. I’ve always had an interest in automating processes and improving efficiencies, and am quick to identify opportunities for automation. The way I see it is – if a particular task has to be performed more than 3 times, and if it is quite predictable – then get it automated – of course, taking into consideration the time, effort and cost.


Being an avid learner, my interests spread into different domains – electronics, networking, data analytics, devops etc. I guess when you’re born into a generation that witnessed the birth of the internet and the subsequent boom in the technology space, you’re primed to be a technology-enthusiast in your later years. I still remember the feeling I had when I first laid eyes on my first computer – a Macintosh – dreaming about the possibilities of this box with a screen. Fast forward a couple of years, and I’d started dismantling electrical and electronic appliances, to learn what made them tick. Pretty soon, I was repairing broken stuff and repurposing old components to create new ‘toys’. Several school years and two college degrees later, I still enjoy learning new things. Learning never stops!
